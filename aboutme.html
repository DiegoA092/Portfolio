About me

Passionate about the intersection of technology and innovation, I am a dedicated Mechatronic Engineer with 9 years of rich experience in the automotive industry. My journey began at Autoliv, where I honed my skills as a Manufacturing Engineer in the Project Launch area. This foundational year was pivotal, setting the stage for my subsequent 8 years at Bosch.

At Bosch, I initially contributed to HVAC motors, which soon led to a unique short-term assignment in Germany. There, I began specializing in coolant valves, eventually becoming the Product Engineer responsible for all coolant valve models across North America sharpening my project management, supplier management and customer service skills.

In January 2024, I transitioned from the automotive sector to embrace a new challenge: software development. Currently being part of the Oracle ONE program and deepening my skills in IT support through a Google IT Support certification, while simultaneously developing a comprehensive database project. T his career shift underscores my commitment to continuous learning and adapting to the dynamic tech landscape.

I am actively seeking job opportunities that will allow me to expand my knowledge and complete my transition to the software development sector.
